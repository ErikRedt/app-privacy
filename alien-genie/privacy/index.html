Alien Genie Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Alien Genie does not track users, show advertising, or sell personal information.

Questions and Answers

Questions are processed on device when Apple Intelligence is available. If Apple Intelligence is unavailable, Alien Genie uses local random fallback answers.

Weather and Location

Weather-related questions may use a named place or your approximate location to fetch weather context through Apple services, including MapKit and WeatherKit. Non-weather questions do not request location.

Reading History

Recent readings may be stored on your device and may sync through your iCloud key-value storage when iCloud is available. You can clear recent readings in the app.

Entertainment Only

Alien Genie is for entertainment only and is not medical, legal, financial, safety, or emergency advice.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the developer through the support link listed on Alien Genie's App Store page.
