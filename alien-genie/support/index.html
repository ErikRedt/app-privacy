Common questions

Why does the Genie say Apple Intelligence is unavailable?
Apple Intelligence requires an iPhone 15 Pro or later (or a compatible Mac/Apple silicon) running a recent iOS/macOS version. On unsupported devices, Alien Genie automatically falls back to classic magic-8-ball style random answers — everything still works.

Why didn't a weather question get a real forecast?
Weather answers use Apple WeatherKit and require a location permission grant. If location access is denied or WeatherKit is temporarily unavailable, the Genie falls back to a regular AI/random answer instead.

How do I restore my Cosmic Pass purchase on a new device?
Open the ⓘ info sheet in the app and tap Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Is my question history private?
Yes. Your reading history syncs only through your own private iCloud account and is never sold, shared, or used for advertising.
